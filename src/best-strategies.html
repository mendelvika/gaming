Betting on sports is an activity that millions of people enjoy in every year, and it has been that way throughout human history. Whether you’re placing a wager on the world cup, or betting on a favorite gladiator thousands of years ago, putting money on this type of event helps to add to the excitement. For many people, betting a little bit of money between friends on a big match is as far as they want to take it. For others, however, betting on sports is a way to make every event engaging (and for still others, it is a way to make an income!).

Bottom Line

No matter why you’re betting on sports, it makes sense to understand the various strategies that one can follow to improve their odds. After all, the main reason to place a wager is to hopefully win! Take a look at the various betting strategies in this article and choose the one that fits best with your situation.

Consider the Moneyline

Experienced sports betters often look at moneyline betting as something only amateurs do, but that is not actually the case. A moneyline bet is just a straight wager on which team is going to win the game. While nothing could be simpler on the surface, there is actually a lot that goes on behind the scenes. People who are just getting started in the world of sports betting will often find that they have a little bit of an advantage in this area, if they do it properly.

When betting on the moneyline, the payout will depend on what the experts believe the odds are of a given team to win. If one team is heavily favored to win, and you bet that they will lose, you can get paid out 2:1, 3:1, or even higher. Since most people who are just getting into sports betting will typically make their first few wagers on their favorite teams, this can be a big advantage.

Bottom Line

If you know every detail about your favorite team including whether players are ‘hot’ at the moment, who is playing injured, and other things, you can make a smart bet. Whether you bet for your team, or against them, your in-depth knowledge can actually put you ahead of the experts in some cases, giving you an opportunity to make money.

Beating the Spread

The next most common betting strategy is to attempt to beat the spread. This can make any game exciting, even if one team is clearly better than the other. On each game, the odds makers will assign an amount by which the winning team is expected to win. For example, in an NBA basketball game between the Chicago Bulls and the Detroit Pistons, the spread may be 10.5 points in favor of the Bulls. If you bet on the bulls, they not only have to be victorious, but they have to win by 11 or more points in order for it to pay out.

Bottom Line

This betting strategy also favors those who have detailed knowledge of one or both teams in the game. When a key player is performing exceptionally well, or has done well against this specific opponent, you may be able to make the right bet to make some money!

Going Against the Crowd

When looking for something that is still fairly easy, but somewhat more advanced than moneyline or beating the spread, going against the crowd can be a lot of fun. This strategy, which is also called ‘Fading the Public’ can be used to wager on just about any event, even if you’re not intimately familiar with the teams playing.

When betting sites set their odds on a game, they look at many factors. Despite popular believe, they aren’t strictly looking at who is most likely to win, and by how much. Another big factor that is considered is what percentage of people will be placing a wager on which team. This is done to help limit the risk of the betting sites in the event of an upset.

Bottom Line

With this in mind, a great strategy is to look at games where most people are going to be betting for one team to win, and then taking the opposite position. This not only makes the game much more exciting, but will actually pay you out better odds than you could otherwise get. For many people, fading the crowd is their first step into more complex betting strategies that look at the science behind betting on sports.

Never Stop Learning

Betting on sports is an exciting activity that millions of people around the world enjoy. Whether you’re just looking to have fun with friends, or you want to turn your love of sports into a potential way to make money, this is a great way to do it. Regardless of your goals, however, it is always important to keep learning about the sports you love, the way sports betting works, human psychology, and many other factors to help ensure the odds are ever in your favor.
