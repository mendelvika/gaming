Available Markets

You will be able to place wagers on a lot of different sporting events when you sign up at Draft Kings. In each of the following markets you can often find multiple different leagues as well, which will help to ensure you can bet on all your favorite teams. The available markets are Aussie rules football, bandy, baseball, basketball, beach volleyball, cricket, curling, cycling, darts, esports, boxing, MMA, football, golf, handball, hockey, horse racing, lacrosse, motor sports, netball, Olympics, rugby, snooker, soccer, speedway, surfing, swimming, tennis, volleyball, and more.

In-Play Betting

If you love making wagers during a live sporting event, you will really enjoy their in-play betting selection. They have tons of different betting options for each game, and they are very easy to find and make. On top of the in-play betting options, Draft Kings has launched a customized type of in-play bet called the flash bet. This gives gamblers options for extremely short-term wagers, which are paid out instantly. The first example of this was in tennis they had flash bets between points to wager on who would score.

Mobile Betting

Mobile betting is fast, safe, and easy when you are on Draft Kings. They have well-designed apps available for Android and Apple devices. Built into the mobile app are all the features that you will find on their main website, which allows you to place your bets and engage in other activities that you enjoy. If you don’t want to use their mobile app, but you are on your phone or tablet, you can also visit their mobile-friendly website.

Promotions & Offers

Draft Kings wants to keep their players engaged and having fun, and one of the ways that they do this is by running various promotions and other offers. There are many different types of promotions based on what sports season it is and other factors. Some promotions are available all the time, such as their sports betting pools. If you are playing on Draft Kings, make sure you check their promotions pages regularly to ensure you are getting all the great benefits.

Deposit Methods

When adding money to your Draft Kings account you can use one of multiple different methods. They will all get the money into your account safely and securely. Most of them will make it so the money is available instantly. The available deposit methods are Visa, Discover, MasterCard, online banking payments, PayPal, Skrill, Play+, and Wire Transfer. They have a maximum deposit limit of $2500 for new customers, but once you go through their verification process you can make deposits that are much larger.

Customer Service

The draft kings support is available 24/7, which is a great benefit for this service. They can be reached via email, filling out a form on their site, or using a live chat feature. Their customer service department is combined with their online casino and fantasy sports pages, but that should not matter. The support team is helpful and friendly whenever you get in touch. You can also visit their FAQ page to get answers to lots of different questions.

Summary

Draft Kings is an excellent platform that has a lot to offer. Their sports booking services are very easy to use and you can wager on tons of different games. If you have used their fantasy sports services, you will definitely want to sign up for the sports betting as well. Even if you are new to online sports betting, however, it makes sense to at least sign up and take advantage of their welcome bonus, which is quite generous.
